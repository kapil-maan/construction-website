Career ⇓

Bolder’s Construction is a great place to work, learn, and grow. We are always looking for talented, passionate, and motivated people to join our team and contribute to our vision of building a better future for everyone. We offer a variety of career opportunities in different fields and levels, such as design, engineering, construction, management, administration, and support. We also provide a competitive compensation and benefits package, as well as a supportive and inclusive work environment. We value diversity, innovation, and excellence in everything we do. We encourage our employees to pursue their professional and personal goals, and to develop their skills and knowledge. We also reward our employees for their performance, achievements, and contributions. Some of the career opportunities that we offer are:-

Design and planning ↣

If you have a creative mind and a keen eye for detail, you can join our design and planning team, where you will be responsible for creating and developing the designs and plans for our projects. You will work with the latest technology and software, such as AutoCAD, Revit, and SketchUp, to produce high-quality and accurate drawings and models. You will also collaborate with other experts, such as architects, engineers, and consultants, to ensure that our projects are feasible, safe, and sustainable. You will need to have a degree or diploma in a relevant field, such as architecture, civil engineering, or interior design, and a portfolio of your previous work.

Engineering ↣

If you have a strong technical background and a problem-solving attitude, you can join our engineering team, where you will be responsible for designing and analyzing the structural components and systems of our projects. You will work with advanced materials and techniques, such as steel, concrete, composite, and prefabricated structures, to optimize the strength, durability, and efficiency of our projects. You will also conduct tests and inspections to ensure the quality and safety of our work. You will need to have a degree or diploma in a relevant field, such as structural engineering, mechanical engineering, or electrical engineering, and a license or certification from a professional body.

Construction and management ↣

If you have a hands-on experience and a leadership skill, you can join our construction and management team, where you will be responsible for executing and managing our projects. You will work with the best materials and equipment available, and supervise the on-site activities of our projects. You will also manage the budget, schedule, and resources of our projects, and communicate with the stakeholders. You will need to have a degree or diploma in a relevant field, such as construction management, project management, or business administration, and a proven track record of successful projects.

Administration and support ↣

If you have a good organizational and communication skill, you can join our administration and support team, where you will be responsible for providing the administrative and logistical support to our projects and operations. You will work with various software and tools, such as Microsoft Office, QuickBooks, and CRM, to handle the documentation, accounting, and customer service of our projects. You will also assist the management and the staff with any other tasks and duties that may arise. You will need to have a degree or diploma in a relevant field, such as administration, finance, or marketing, and a customer-oriented mindset.

Vacation and leave ➣

Training and development ➣
We offer a variety of training and development opportunities that help you enhance your skills and knowledge, and advance your career. We also offer tuition reimbursement, mentoring, and coaching programs that support your professional and personal growth.
Recognition and reward ➣
We offer a recognition and reward program that acknowledges and appreciates your hard work and dedication. We also offer awards, incentives, and perks that celebrate your achievements and contributions.
