Media ⇩

Bolder’s Construction is proud to share some of the media coverage and recognition that we have received for our projects and achievements. We are grateful for the support and appreciation that we have received from the public, the media, and the industry. Here are some of the media highlights that feature our work:-

The Skyline Tower featured in The Times of India :-

The Skyline Tower, our 50-story mixed-use development in New Delhi, was featured in The Times of India, one of the leading newspapers in the country. The article praised the project for its design, engineering, and environmental performance, and called it a “masterpiece of modern architecture”. The article also quoted our CEO, who said that the project was a “dream come true” for him and his team.
You can read the full article here:- The Skyline Tower: A masterpiece of modern architecture!!! .

The Green Valley School awarded the Green Building Award :-

The Green Valley School, our state-of-the-art educational facility in Hyderabad, was awarded the Green Building Award by the Ministry of Environment and Forests, for its efforts in reducing the environmental impact of its construction and operation. The award ceremony was held in New Delhi, and was attended by our project manager, who received the award from the Minister of Environment and Forests. The award recognized the project for its bioclimatic design, natural ventilation system, waste management system, and organic farm.
You can view the full article of the award ceremony here:- Green Building Award Ceremony!!! .

The Bolder Bridge featured in National Geographic :-

The Bolder Bridge, our 2-km long cable-stayed bridge in Mumbai, was featured in National Geographic, one of the most prestigious magazines in the world. The article explored the engineering and aesthetic aspects of the bridge, and how it has transformed the cityscape and the mobility of the people. The article also included stunning photographs of the bridge, taken by our photographer, who captured the bridge from different angles and times of the day.
You can view the full article here:-The Bolder Bridge:- A symbol of progress and connectivity .
