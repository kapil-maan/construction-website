History/ About Us ⇩

Bolder’s Construction is a leading company in the field of building and engineering. We have been delivering high-quality projects across the country since 1998, with a focus on innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

➵ Our mission is to create structures that enhance the lives of people and communities. We believe that every project is an opportunity to showcase our skills, creativity, and values. We work with a diverse team of experts, from architects and engineers to contractors and suppliers, to ensure that every aspect of our work meets the highest standards of excellence.

➵ We have completed over 500 projects in various sectors, such as residential, commercial, industrial, educational, and healthcare.


Some of our notable projects include :-

➾ The Skyline Tower, a 50-story mixed-use development in New Delhi, featuring luxury apartments, offices, retail, and a hotel.
➾ The Green Valley School, a state-of-the-art educational facility in Hyderabad, designed to promote environmental awareness and social responsibility among students and staff.
➾ The Bolder Bridge, a 2-km long cable-stayed bridge in Mumbai, connecting the eastern and western suburbs and reducing traffic congestion.

We are proud of our achievements and awards, such as :-

➾ The Best Construction Company of the Year, awarded by the Indian Construction Association in 2020.
➾ The Green Building Award, given by the Ministry of Environment and Forests in 2019, for our efforts in reducing the environmental impact of our projects.
➾ The Customer Choice Award, voted by the public in 2018, for our outstanding service and quality.
