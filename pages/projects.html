Projects ⇓

Bolder’s Construction has completed over 500 projects in various sectors, such as residential, commercial, industrial, educational, and healthcare. We have delivered high-quality projects across the country, with a focus on innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Here are some of our featured projects that showcase our skills, creativity, and values:-

The Skyline Tower ⇢

A 50-story mixed-use development in New Delhi, featuring luxury apartments, offices, retail, and a hotel. The project was completed in 2021, and has won several awards for its design, engineering, and environmental performance. The project features a unique curved facade, a rooftop garden, a solar power system, and a rainwater harvesting system. The project was a challenging one, as it involved working in a densely populated and congested area, and required high standards of safety and quality. We used innovative techniques, such as modular construction, prefabrication, and 3D printing, to reduce the construction time and cost, and to minimize the environmental impact. The project has become a landmark in the city, and has received positive feedback from the residents, tenants, and visitors.

The Green Valley School ⇢

A state-of-the-art educational facility in Hyderabad, designed to promote environmental awareness and social responsibility among students and staff. The project was completed in 2020, and has been recognized as a model of green education. The project features a bioclimatic design, a natural ventilation system, a waste management system, and a organic farm. The project was a rewarding one, as it involved working with the local community, and required a high level of collaboration and communication. We used sustainable materials, such as bamboo, mud, and recycled plastic, to create a low-carbon and low-waste building, and to enhance the aesthetic and cultural value. The project has become a source of inspiration and learning for the students, staff, and parents.

The Bolder Bridge ⇢

A 2-km long cable-stayed bridge in Mumbai, connecting the eastern and western suburbs and reducing traffic congestion. The project was completed in 2019, and has been praised for its structural beauty and efficiency. The project features a slender steel deck, a single pylon, and a dynamic lighting system. The project was a complex one, as it involved working in a harsh and unpredictable environment, and required a high level of precision and accuracy. We used advanced technology, such as drones, sensors, and robots, to monitor and control the construction process, and to ensure the safety and quality of the work. The project has become a symbol of progress and connectivity for the city, and has received appreciation from the public and the authorities.
